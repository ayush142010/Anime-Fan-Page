Your Name

"Your Name" is a Japanese animated romantic fantasy film directed by Makoto Shinkai, released in 2016. It blends romance, time travel, fate, and
 stunning visuals into an emotional and unforgettable story.

🎬 Basic Info:

🎞 Title: Your Name (Japanese: 君の名は / Kimi no Na wa)
🎨 Director & Writer: Makoto Shinkai
🧑‍🎤 Music: RADWIMPS (famous Japanese rock band)
⏳ Genres: Romance, Drama, Supernatural, Time-travel, Coming-of-age

📖 Plot Summary (No Major Spoilers):

Two teenagers —
🌆 Taki (a high school boy in Tokyo)
🏞 Mitsuha (a girl living in a rural village named Itomori) —
suddenly begin switching bodies without warning. As they try to understand and communicate through messages and actions, a mysterious connection
forms between them. But there's more to their bond than just a supernatural twist — time, memory, and fate are all intertwined in ways they never
expected.

💔 Themes in Your Name:

Destiny and fate (運命)
Love that transcends time and space
The connection between people and places
Japanese cultural traditions (like musubi, red cords, and shrines)
The fragility of memory

🖼️ Why it’s so Popular:

✨ Stunning animation & visuals (skies, Tokyo, comets, nature)
🎶 Powerful and emotional soundtrack by RADWIMPS
💔 Heartfelt story that makes you laugh, cry, and think deeply
🌍 Broke records globally — one of the highest-grossing anime films ever

💬 Famous Lines (English):

“I’m always searching for something, for someone.”
“Even if I forget you… I’ll remember the feeling.”
“What’s your name?”
🇯🇵 Famous Lines (Japanese):
“ずっと何かを、誰かを、探している。”
(Zutto nanika o, dareka o, sagashiteiru.)
“名前は…？”
(Namae wa...?) — “Your name is…?”

🏆 Awards & Recognition:

Over $380 million at the global box office
Won the Japan Academy Prize for Best Screenplay
Listed in Time Magazine’s Top 10 Movies of 2017
Considered a modern anime classic
